Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I download App Jump?

A: App Jump is available on the App Store. Simply search for "App Jump" and tap the download button.

Q: Is App Jump free to play?

A: Yes, App Jump is free to download and play. There are optional in-app purchases available.

Q: How do I report a bug?

A: If you encounter a bug, please email us at bugs@appjump.art with a description of the issue and steps to reproduce it.
