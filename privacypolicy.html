Privacy Policy for App Jump

Last Updated: October 22, 2024

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how App Jump ("we," "our," or "us") handles information when you use our mobile game application ("App Jump" or the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our App.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally

  • Game progress and scores
  • Game settings and preferences

We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Camera or microphone access
  • Photos or media files
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Analytics data
  • Crash reports or debugging information

How We Use Information

We use the locally stored information solely to:

  • Save your game progress
  • Maintain your game preferences
  • Enable gameplay functionality

Data Storage and Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No cloud storage or backup is provided

Third-Party Services

Our App may use:

  • Apple Game Center (optional) - for leaderboards and achievements

Please refer to Apple's privacy policy for information about their data practices.

Children's Privacy

Our App does not collect personal information from any users, including children under 13. All game data remains strictly on the device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Reset game progress through device settings
  • Opt-out of Game Center features
  • Delete the App and all locally stored data
